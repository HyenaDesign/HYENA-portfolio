FIRE MOTION LOGO 2024

INTRODUCTION

This project was an assignment for Design 4 in my second year of my professional bachelor. Since motion design was trending, we had to make our own motion logo with your given word. My word was "FIRE".

DESIGN EXECUTION

The first thing was sketching some logos that represented the word "FIRE". I quickly sketched an "I" as a candle with the dot being a flame. That was a little bit too simple in my opinion. So then i chose to make every letter its own seperate flame.

Then i designed the animation in Figma and exported each frame with the different flames. I added some glow to make it look like light emission in the dark. I still thought it was too boring so I added some looping foreground of smoke.
