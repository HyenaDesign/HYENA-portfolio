UNTOX 2023

INTRODUCTION

This project aims to improve the gaming community and reduce toxicity amongst gamers. With a focus on social media, reviews, and meeting new players, our goal is to create a seamless and pleasant experience for gamers across all platforms.

CONCEPT DEVELOPMENT

The whole concept is creating a fun experience for all gamers. Using a social media type platform, creating new ways to meet other gamers. When you played with someone and you enjoyed the session, you can leave a positive review on their profiles. More reviews creates an overview on reputation. That way people know if they're enjoyable to game wit or not.

DESIGN EXECUTION

I wanted to make the UI and UX as an invitation to a user-friendly platform. Using a border-radius to create some peace and comfort. The fonts I chose are bold to show the gaming aspect. The color palette exists out of multiple vibrant colors. Green to show the toxicity that needs to be cleared. A vibrant light-blue to show the gaming aesthetic. Dark grey-scale colors for the dark-mode and minimalism. The logo is made in Illustrator by experimenting with multiple bold fonts. I typed out the name "UNTOX" (Untox for detox, untoxic) and quickly made a slash through the middle with the vibrant green and dark blue colors. The light blue is used for accents and to visualise active elements in the UI.

PROTOTYPING AND DEVELOPMENT

For prototyping I used Figma. I started off with minimal wireframes to create a flow and lay-out in the app. I made sure there was enough elements in the app to personalise to your preferences. When the wireframes were finished, I started on the full prototype. Filling in the blank spaces and lorem ipsum with real content. Integrating all fonts and colors and quickly UNTOX was finished. I did some tweaking and moving some elements to make it a clean and sleek design. The stock images I used are from Unsplash. Then it was time to simulate a few screens in HTML, CSS and JS to test it with a mobile device.
