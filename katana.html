KATANA INDUSTRIES

INTRODUCTION

This project is called "KATANA INDUSTRIES". It's a branding for a fictive car import business. I made it because I wanted to practice my logo designing skills. A fun fact, this branding was made in 2 hours whilst in class. But except branding, there is not much to it. It's just a little side project.

CONCEPT DEVELOPMENT

I came up with the idea because I'm interested in cars. I used to study Car Mechanics. And in that time I made this branding, I was helping a friend with his car tuning. So I just decided one day to create a branding for a non-existing thing.

DESIGN EXECUTION

I just opened up Adobe Illustrator and started drawing some lines and experimenting with different fonts. Then suddenly the name "KATANA INDUSTRIES" came to mind. I chose a sleek font and wrote out the name. Quickly the logo was finished. After that I just played with some few colors and tweeked the logo a bit to fit the japanese import style. And tadaa!
